How this works: With interest deductibility, your rental taxable income = (rental income − all deductible expenses including interest). Without it, taxable income = (rental income − non-interest expenses only). The difference in tax payable at your marginal rate is your saving. Since 1 April 2025, residential rental property interest is fully deductible again.
